# Create: Biotech — Player Introduction

[English](INTRODUCTION.md) · [中文](INTRODUCTION.zh-CN.md) · [README](../README.md) · [中文 README](../README.zh-CN.md)

> A Create addon where the machines are alive.

## Philosophy

Create gave us a world made of cogs, belts, and stress — beautifully mechanical, but beautifully **sterile**. Create: Biotech asks a different question:

> What if the next gear in your factory wasn't iron, but **alive**?

Slimes can roll. Magma cubes burn. Ghasts float. Squids ink. Cats collapse wave-functions. Every one of those is a mechanism waiting to happen — you just have to treat it as a part instead of a pest.

So this mod plants living creatures right inside the kinetic network. Not as decoration, not as a separate side-system, but as components that obey stress, ride belts, get processed in basins, and fit into contraptions the same way a brass funnel does. Biology becomes engineering. The Overworld becomes your supply chain.

Three ideas hold the whole thing together:

- **Mobs are mechanisms.** A small slime isn't a drop source — it *is* the belt surface. A ghast isn't a mob to kill — it *is* the lift. A cat isn't a pet — it *is* a quantum coin-flip.
- **Acquisition is interaction.** Most parts can't be crafted out of thin air. You have to meet the creature, capture it, feed it, or coax it. The factory begins in the wild.
- **Each living component does something dead metal can't.** Biological alternatives aren't reskins — they have their own quirks, costs, and edges that pure Create machinery cannot reach.

## What's inside

You'll build factories that:

- **Move things with living transport.** Slime belts loop endlessly. Magma belts torch what they carry. Power belts turn a sprinting player into stress.
- **Cook entities, not just items.** Drop a small slime into a basin and watch the mechanical press treat it like any other ingredient.
- **Lift, sail, and roam.** Strap a saddle onto a ghast and turn it into a hot-air balloon contraption you can fly.
- **Print books with squids.** Feed an enchanted book in, get a copy out — ink courtesy of the resident cephalopod.
- **Harvest experience as a material.** Pump XP, crystallize it, grow it on budding blocks, store it as a fluid.
- **Tame chaos.** A creeper blast chamber that contains explosions. An evoker that performs enchanting rituals on command. A Schrödinger's cat that emits redstone only when nobody is looking.
- **Get help from the wildlife.** A fixed carrot fishing rod that auto-attracts animals. A spider assembly table that weaves things in silk. Cardboard boxes that let you pick mobs up and carry them home.

…and a long tail of smaller parts — universal joints, bone ratchets, slime clutches, buffer pads, explosion-proof vaults — that exist so the living machines have somewhere to plug in.

You don't need to memorize any of this. Open the Create: Biotech creative tab in JEI, click a block, and Ponder will show you how it works in animation. Everything is meant to be discovered the same way Create itself is: by tinkering.

## Getting started

1. Install Create 6.0.8 for Minecraft 1.20.1 (Forge 47.1.33). Optional: JEI, Jade.
2. Drop the jar in your `mods/` folder.
3. Go find a slime. The factory starts there.
